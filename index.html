Frame: The main structural component of the bike, which supports all other parts and provides the basic shape of the bike.

Wheels:The two round structures that support the bike and enable it to move. Each wheel consists of a rim, spokes, and a hub.

Tires: The rubber outer layer of the wheels that make contact with the ground.

Pedals: The part of the bike that the rider uses to propel the bike forward by pushing down with their feet.
